Hi. I'm Johanne Antoine.

I am graduate student at Tufts University with an interest in game programming. I graduated from Boston University with a major in computer science.

When I was in high school, I learned about the Hour of Code from Google, and was hooked. Subsequently, I joined the local GirlsWhoCode club at my school, where I learned how to create small programs with Python. From that point, I knew that I wanted to pursue a career in computer science.

Right now, I am helping my dad with his website. I have experience in Unreal Engine (C++), Godot, Ren'py, and Python. Previously, I had an externship at IBM, and an internship at Google.
